Aristotle’s writings contained cover logic, metaphysics, physics, psychology, ethics, politics, the arts, literature, biology, and more. In each area Aristotle introduced a new methodology for rational inquiry and investigation that left its mark to this day.

Perhaps Aristotle’s most admired and wondrous works are those in metaphysics, logic, and ethics for the depth of abstract intellectual analysis the philosopher achieved. Aristotle was truly the first teacher of systematic and rigorous thinking for humanity.

This complete collection of Aristotle’s works represents an invaluable resource for anyone wishing to delve into the mind of this immortal philosopher and understand his methodology of philosophical inquiry and thought.
