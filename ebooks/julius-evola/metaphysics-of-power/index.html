Metaphysics of Power

Julius Evola

politics, philosophy.

In his book Metaphysics of Power, Julius Evola examines the concept of power from a metaphysical traditionalist perspective far from modern political conceptions.

Evola sees true power as deriving from a transcendent source beyond the material world. Power in its essence is spiritual, related to the individual’s ability to connect with the Absolute and embody its principles. Hence its legitimacy regardless of outward means.

Evola also associates authentic power with sacred traditions and rituals that give a spiritual dimension to the ruler. For him, power is not just a means of control but transcends materiality toward a lofty spiritual aim inspired by the eternal cosmic order.
