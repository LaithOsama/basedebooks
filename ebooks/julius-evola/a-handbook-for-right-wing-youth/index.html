A Handbook for Right-Wing Youth

Julius Evola

politics philosophy

“a political and ideological guide aimed at the younger generation with right-wing leanings. Evola outlines his views on traditionalism, anti-modernism, and the preservation of hierarchical social structures.”

view in arabic download section

A Handbook for Right-Wing Youth offers guidance and advice for right-leaning youth on defending their values and beliefs in a contemporary world they see as hostile to them.

It urges right-wing youth to uphold traditional values like national identity, family, and order, warning them against drifting toward liberalism and tolerance of right-wing enemies. It also suggests ways to spread right-wing ideas and influence society.

Despite espousing hardline stances, the book carries motivational messages for right-wing youth to remain committed to their convictions in the face of perceived leftist and liberal ideological dominance.
