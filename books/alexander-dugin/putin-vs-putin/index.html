Putin vs Putin

Alexander Dugin

politics history biography

view in arabic download section

In his book Putin vs Putin, Russian thinker Alexander Dugin examines President Vladimir Putin’s political trajectory and analyzes the contradictions in his policies and ideology.

Dugin sees two different Putins: the conservative Putin who represents traditional values, and the liberal Putin open to the West. He believes the tension between these two faces is key to understanding inconsistencies in Russian politics.

Dugin also criticizes some of Putin’s foreign policy and economic choices, arguing Putin has so far failed to offer an alternative civilizational project for Russia beyond the Western model. Despite criticisms, Dugin sees Putin as remaining the best option to lead Russia moving forward.
