Mission Statement

An independent, non-profit, public domain digital library. We are carrying this project for the betterment of our Arab-Islamic nation, in the hope that it will serve the community and become a permanent charitable institution within the reach of all, and it will be an ongoing charity for everyone who participates in it.

Recognizing the limitations of existing Arabic digital libraries—often flooded with low-quality content riddled with ads and stymied by poor programming—I have endeavored to create a library that aspires to:

  • Offer Arabic translations, accompanied by their English counterparts, ensuring readers are shielded from substandard translations.
  • Present books that foster cognitive awareness, broaden horizons, and move beyond unhelpful novels and self-help works.
  • Emphasize books that support tradition while discouraging progress, with particular attention to religious literature.
  • Implement optimal coding solutions to deliver a swift, streamlined site experience. Adopting leading-edge technologies, we eschew disruptive ads and superfluous code, preserving an uncluttered, seamless reading environment.
  • Prioritize ease of use and speed for all. Enabling universal access to knowledge drives our approach. We aim to empower engagement without overwhelming non-technical users with needless complexity.
  • Put effort into professional-quality typography: use “curly” quotes, details like em- and en-dashes, and ensure that our typography meet a professional-grade and consistent typographical standard.

A Community-Driven Project

Our endeavor is built upon the collective efforts of a community united in the pursuit of knowledge and literacy.

Contributions is the beating heart of our project - where your contributions and volunteering efforts make all the difference. As a sole individual, I lack the resources to make this project truly impactful.

There are numerous opportunities for volunteers to get involved, some of which include:

  1. Translation: The thoughtful review and translation of texts represents a pivotal foundation of our project's efforts. This undertaking requires careful consideration in selecting the optimal AI model for generating preliminary translations—establishing initial groundwork that we subsequently refine into fully realized editions.
  2. Texts and Summaries: Our commitment to enhancing content extends across mediums—from book summaries to transcripts and more. AI generates a substantial portion of this content. We invite you to share any summaries, critiques, or analyses of the works we offer. Any contributions of value will find a home integrated seamlessly into the respective work's presentation.
  3. Curriculum Suggestions: In pursuing knowledge, one often encounters resources both fruitful and lacking. We appreciate the value of a carefully structured curriculum. Please suggest supplemental materials—readings, lectures, or other resources—to enrich our offerings. Help shape the path for others, steering them toward meaningful insights and away from unnecessary detours.
  4. Article Submissions or Transfers: We welcome submissions of original articles and timeless content from other sources. When possible, we aim to provide audio versions to widen accessibility. Our emphasis lies not merely in the quality of content but also in its aesthetic presentation and enduring relevance.
  5. Proofreading: For those with an eye for details, consider lending your talents to identify and correct errors in spelling, grammar, formatting, and style. We aim to develop streamlined, volunteer-driven methods for refinement and invite you to participate when ready.

While the specifics of contribution remain in flux as we balance ideals of quality, efficiency, and experience, know that your dedication to improving our shared resources is deeply appreciated. Once finalized, submission will be possible via email. For now, we welcome your thoughts and suggestions as we shape an accessible platform built on knowledge in community.

What Sets Us Apart?

Unlike platforms offering indiscriminate content, we are singularly devoted to delivering remarkable books of the highest caliber—presented with care to provide readers an enriching experience and comprehensive understanding.

Our undertaking extends beyond a mere repository of titles. We aim to establish a hub for thoughtful translation, curated learning pathways, preservation of articles, and an unparalleled aesthetic presentation.

Translation?!

We are actively working on an ambitious translation initiative to unlock books long obscured from Arab audiences. Leveraging artificial intelligence for initial drafts and meticulous human refinement, we can produce nuanced, contextually accurate renditions at remarkable speed. By bridging linguistic and cultural divides, this effort expands access to ideas that have remained in obscurity for too long.

Empowering Knowledge Acquisition

The quest for knowledge can prove challenging, with countless titles and unclear directions. Our primary aim is to provide structured learning for maximal growth. By curating materials and illuminating connections, we hope to inspire purposeful engagement with the life of the mind.

Our Vision for Articles

Our ultimate vision is a comprehensive library—a vast resource to aid students of science and thought. We recognize that articles can have an outsized impact on intellectual and personal growth.

To facilitate article contributions, we aim to create an intuitive submission process accessible even to non-technical writers. For now, articles may be shared via email. Our emphasis is on collecting timeless wisdom rather than chasing ephemera.

Inspiration from “Standard Ebooks”

The exemplary “Standard Ebooks” project has significantly influenced our design philosophy and commitment to quality. As we craft a distinctive identity tailored to our aims, their example continues to inspire. We remain focused on developing programming, content, and structure, and later dedicating time to refine aesthetics and presentation. With experience, I hope to cultivate competence across all facets of this undertaking.

Why Not Fork “Standard Ebooks”?

Forking “Standard Ebooks” project would require considerable time and effort to adapt to their specific implementation, which may not align with our distinct objectives. Instead, we view this as an invaluable learning experience, as the project has been instrumental in my personal growth and development.
I must also emphasize that without the existence of the "Standard Ebooks" project, this website would not have come into being. Irrespective of the scenario, it stands as a remarkable initiative that truly warrants financial donate.
